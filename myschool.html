The History of Koforidua Secondary Technical School (KSTS)

The school was established in the 1967 by Dr.Kwame Nkrumah.It was the second secondary technical after Ghana Secondary Technical School (GSTS) in Takoradi.The school is known as an all boys school

For more information you can visit KSTS online for more details.
